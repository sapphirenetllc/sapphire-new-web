Our Story

Launched in 2025 with a clear mission: to help consumers navigate the overwhelming landscape of internet, cable, and streaming options with independent, unbiased guidance. We recognized that people needed a trusted resource—free from provider affiliations—to help them make informed connectivity decisions.

We saw that many consumers felt confused by countless provider options, conflicting information, and aggressive sales tactics. Our founders created Sapphire Internet as an independent assistance platform where clients receive honest guidance, personalized support, and clear information to help them choose the right services for their needs.

Today, we're proud to have assisted thousands of clients across the country, earning trust through transparent communication and genuine care. Our commitment remains steadfast: providing expert guidance and support with the independence, transparency, and personal attention that only a truly unbiased platform can deliver.
