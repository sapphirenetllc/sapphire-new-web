Our Story

Why We Exist

We launched in 2025 with a clear mission: help consumers navigate the overwhelming landscape of internet, cable, and streaming options with independent, unbiased guidance. We recognised that people needed a trusted resource — free from provider affiliations — to make informed connectivity decisions.

Many consumers felt confused by countless provider options, conflicting information, and aggressive sales tactics. Our founders created Sapphire Internet as an independent assistance platform where clients receive honest guidance, personalised support, and clear information.

Today, we've assisted thousands of clients across the country, earning trust through transparent communication and genuine care.
